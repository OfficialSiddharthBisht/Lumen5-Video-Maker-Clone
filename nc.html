Challenge

Make videos like a pro (affordable, engaging and in less time)

The University’s social media team did not have the bandwidth to shoot high-end videos, so they were working with a videographer who trained them in using Adobe After Effects and Premiere. But creating and editing videos was taking too much of their time—they needed to free up their schedules and deliver projects faster. That’s not all. Metrics showed their social media videos were not always performing well. Since one of their main goals was to raise the number of views, shares, likes, and comments, they were missing a robust tool they could use to churn out text-based videos and better engage their audiences.

"Spending half of a business day to create and complete a video project? We can’t afford that."


Drew Sykes, Social Media Director

Solution

Lumen5 and NC State University: a match made in heaven.

When NC State University found Lumen5, it was love at first sight. In the beginning, NC State University was happy with generating attractive text-based videos in a timely fashion. With Lumen5 regularly releasing new features, they were able to add sound bites, use different cropping options and pick layouts that match their branding. The tool’s availability goes hand in hand with the social media team’s needs. If there's an event on campus, they can shoot directly with their phones and create videos on the go. Plus, Lumen5’s features allows them to produce content they can repurpose for other platforms. Lumen5’s little tweaks are improving NC State University’s social media game, allowing them to stay on brand and level up their creativity.

Results

Increased reach, increased engagement, and time saved.

Using Lumen5 allowed NC State University to increase its number of videos created and in less time.
