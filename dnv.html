About DNV

Building the invisible infrastructure of trust

DNV was founded 150 years ago in Norway as a ship classification society. Over the years, it expanded to industries such as oil and gas, renewables, automotive and aerospace, food and beverage, healthcare, and others. They provide a full range of services, including advisory, certification, data and analytics, inspection, testing, training, and verification—to name a few. Today, DNV GL is a B2B global leader in quality assurance and risk management and on a mission to help companies become safe, smarter, and greener.

The Challenge

Turn lengthy content into catchy videos.

When DNV GL decided to invest more in their digital presence, they realized video content would make their social media accounts stand out. They also had lengthy, influential reports they needed to turn into engaging videos without spending a fortune on video specialists. So, they started producing video content in-house. However, the whole process of downloading stock videos, exporting them to Adobe Premiere, editing and sharing them with the team was taking a lot of time. Plus, many team members were hesitant about their video making skills.
